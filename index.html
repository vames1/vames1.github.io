About Me

I'm Victor Oluwaseyi Akindiose, a passionate Cloud Engineer based in Lagos, Nigeria. I specialise in building, automating and deploying scalable cloud infrastructure on AWS using modern DevOps tools and best practices.

With a background in UI/UX Design, I bring a unique perspective to cloud engineering — combining technical expertise with an eye for clean, well-structured and efficient systems. I transitioned into cloud engineering driven by a deep passion for building reliable infrastructure that powers real-world applications at scale.

I am currently completing my Cloud Engineering certification with AltSchool Africa, where I have gained extensive hands-on experience deploying real infrastructure on AWS. My work spans Terraform, Docker, Kubernetes, Ansible, CI/CD pipelines, CloudWatch monitoring and more.

I am actively seeking Cloud Engineer and DevOps Engineer opportunities where I can contribute my skills, grow professionally and help teams build world-class cloud infrastructure. I am hardworking, detail-oriented and committed to continuous learning.
